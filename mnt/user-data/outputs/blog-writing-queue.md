## WRITING GUIDELINES (For All Articles)

### Structure
- **H1:** Article title (one per page)
- **H2:** Main sections (4-8 per article)
- **H3:** Subsections as needed
- Short paragraphs (2-4 sentences)
- Bullet points for lists
- Bold for emphasis on key terms

### SEO Requirements
- Include primary keyword in H1, first paragraph, and at least one H2
- Use related keywords naturally throughout
- Meta description: 150-160 characters including primary keyword
- Internal links: Minimum 3-5 per article
- External links: Only to authoritative sources if needed

### Content Requirements
- Accurate, evidence-based information
- Warm, empathetic tone
- Second person ("you")
- Acknowledge difficulty while offering hope
- Include practical, actionable advice
- End with CHC CTA

### CTAs
Each article should include:
- Mid-article CTA (after 50-60% of content)
- End-of-article CTA
- CTA text should relate to article topic
- Link to /therapist-matching

### Schema
- Use Article schema (already in template)
- Include datePublished and dateModified
